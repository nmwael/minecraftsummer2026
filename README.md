# Summer 2026 Survival

A curated Minecraft **26.1.2** modpack for family-friendly cooperative play — emphasizing exploration, village enhancement, and decoration.

## Players

- **Child A** — Exploration & village enhancement (new biomes, structures, villagers)
- **Child B** — Exploration & creative building with decoration
- **Parent** — Wants stable, polished experience with JourneyMap

## Requirements

- Minecraft **26.1.2**
- Mod loader: **NeoForge 26.1.2**
- **Java 25** (required by Minecraft 26.x)
- **JourneyMap** (required)

## Pack Philosophy

- **Stability first** — well-maintained, compatible mods only
- **Family-friendly** — age-appropriate content
- **Performance** — native Sodium + Lithium on NeoForge
- **Cooperative** — works great on LAN / small servers
